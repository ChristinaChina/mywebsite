# Ingredients
  - 500 g beef brisket
  - 2 potatoes
  - 2 carrots
  - Light soy sauce
  - Salt
  - Cooking oil (optional)
  - Water

# Instructions
## Remove the beef smell
  - Cut the beef into large chunks
  - Put the beef into a pot with cold water (no seasoning)
  - Bring to a boil and cook for 3–5 minutes
  - Skim off the foam, remove the beef, and rinse with warm water
*This step is very important for clean flavor*
## Simmer the beef
  - Add a little oil to the pot (or skip the oil)
  - Add the beef and lightly stir
  - Add water to cover the beef
  - Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat
  - Simmer for 1.5–2 hours 
## Add vegetables
  - Cut potatoes and carrots into chunks
  - When the beef is tender, add the vegetables
  - Add 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  - Simmer for 15–20 minutes until soft
## Season and serve
   - Taste the soup
   - Add salt if needed
   - Serve hot

# Result
   - Clean, natural beef flavor
   - Soft potatoes that slightly thicken the soup
   - Light, comforting, and not greasy
